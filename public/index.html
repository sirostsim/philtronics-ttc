      Operator Productivity Rate

      Active timer time as a percentage of available working hours, after deducting breaks. Mon–Thu 8h productive, Fri 5h productive.

      Assembly Build Times

      Groups timer records by Works Order and Route Card number. Shows individual operator contributions, combined operator-hours, and elapsed wall-clock time per assembly. Multi-operator assemblies are highlighted.

      Quality & Right First Time

      Right First Time rate based on assemblies with a Works Order number. An assembly fails RFT if any timer on it is logged as a Re-Work Request. Rework hours are shown separately from standard production time.
