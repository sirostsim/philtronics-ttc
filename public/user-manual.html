What is Work Time?

Work Time is a shopfloor timing system built for Philtronics Ltd. When you start working on an assembly job, you press START. When you finish, you press STOP. The system records exactly how long the job took, which workstation it was done on, and which work order it belongs to.

This information helps the production team track performance, spot bottlenecks, and plan the day more accurately.

💡
No technical knowledge needed.

If you can use a smartphone, you can use Work Time. Everything is done with large, clearly labelled buttons on a tablet screen.

User Roles

Every user has a role that controls what they can see and do. Your role is assigned by your administrator.

👷
Operator
  • Start and stop your own timers
  • View your own job history
  • Cancel your own timer within 60 seconds
🦺
Supervisor
  • Everything Operators can do
  • View all operators' history
  • Cancel or adjust any timer
  • View the Wall Board
📊
Manager
  • Everything Supervisors can do
  • View the Dashboard
  • Export data to spreadsheet
🔧
Administrator
  • Everything Managers can do
  • Create and edit user accounts
  • Reset passwords
  • Delete timer records
ℹ️
Not sure what role you have?

After signing in, your name and role appear in the top-right corner of every screen.

Form Fields Explained

FieldRequired?What to enter
Item Number✅ RequiredThe part or assembly number you are working on. Letters, numbers, hyphens and underscores only, maximum 40 characters. Can be scanned from a barcode or QR code.
WorkstationOptionalThe workstation or bench where the job is being done (e.g. WS-01, BENCH-3). Can be scanned. Helps managers see which stations are busy and appears in all history views and exports.
W/O NumberOptionalThe Work Order number this job belongs to (e.g. WO-10042). Can be scanned from a works order document. Appears in history and CSV exports for traceability.
Time CheckOptional tick boxMarks this as a measured timing rather than a simple time task. Tick it when management needs a formal recorded result. Appears as a blue ✓ Time Check badge on all history entries and the Wall Board.

Scanning a Barcode or QR Code

Every field with a ⊞ Scan button can be filled by scanning a barcode or QR code using the tablet's camera. This is faster and avoids typing errors.

1
Tap the Scan button next to the field you want to fill

The camera screen opens automatically.

2
Allow camera access (first time only)

Chrome will ask if the website can use your camera. Tap Allow. You will not be asked again on this device.

3
Point the camera at the barcode or QR code

Hold the tablet steady with the code between the corner markers on screen. The system reads it automatically within a second or two. Good lighting helps.

4
The field fills in automatically

The camera closes and the field is filled with the scanned value. Check it looks correct then continue as normal.

💡
Torch button

In low light a 🔦 Torch button appears at the bottom of the camera screen. Tap it to turn on the tablet's flash as a light.

ℹ️
Scanning works for all three fields.

Item Number, Workstation, and W/O Number all have Scan buttons. Item Number only accepts a valid item number format. Workstation and W/O Number accept any barcode content.

This appears at the top of every screen while a timer is running — even if you navigate away. It is a reminder that your timer is still going.

Cancelling a Timer

If you started a timer by mistake, you can cancel it.

Within 60 seconds of starting

Tap the small Cancel button next to STOP. A confirmation box appears — tap Cancel Timer to confirm or Keep Running to go back.

After 60 seconds

Only a Supervisor or above can cancel the timer. Ask your supervisor — they must enter a reason, which is permanently stored in the audit trail.

🔒
All cancellations are permanently recorded.

Who cancelled it, when, and the reason given are stored in the audit trail and cannot be deleted.

Viewing Your History

Tap ☰ → History to see your own completed jobs. The default view shows the last 7 days. Use the date pickers to change the range and tap Search.

Each entry shows the item number, workstation, W/O number, start and end times, and duration. A blue ✓ Time Check badge appears on entries that were marked as measured timings.

Supervisor: Cancelling or Adjusting a Timer

As a Supervisor you can cancel or adjust any timer — including those over 60 seconds old. A reason is always required and is permanently recorded in the audit trail.

🔒
All changes are permanently recorded.

Your name, the date and time, and the reason you gave are stored permanently.

Manager: Exporting to CSV

On the Dashboard, set your filters and tap ⬇ Export CSV. The file downloads to your device and can be opened in Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets.

The export includes all fields: Item Number, Operator Name, Workstation, W/O Number, Start and End times (both UTC and London time), Duration in seconds and minutes, Time Check flag, and Status.

💡
The file opens correctly in Excel.

The CSV includes special formatting so times and dates display correctly without any manual changes needed.

3

💡
Disabling an account

Tap Edit next to the user's name, untick Account Active, and tap Save Changes. They can no longer sign in but their history records are kept.

Administrator: Deleting a Timer Record

If a timer record needs to be permanently removed — for example a stuck active timer that cannot be stopped — go to History, find the record, and tap the 🗑 button on the right side of the entry.

⚠️
Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

The timer record and its full audit log are removed from the database. Only delete records that are genuinely incorrect or stuck. A confirmation box will appear before anything is deleted.

💡
Finding stuck timers to delete

In History, set the Status dropdown to Active (stuck timers) and tap Search. Any timer that is stuck as active will appear, with the 🗑 delete button visible.

Troubleshooting

I cannot sign in

  • Check your username and password are typed correctly
  • Passwords are case-sensitive — check caps lock is not on
  • After 10 failed attempts, wait 15 minutes before trying again
  • If your account may have been disabled, contact your administrator

The Scan button is not working

  • Make sure you are using Google Chrome on your Android tablet
  • Chrome version 83 or later is required (most devices already have this)
  • If you previously denied camera permission, tap the padlock icon in the address bar, find Camera, change it to Allow, then refresh the page
  • As an alternative, type the value manually

My active timer is not showing after a refresh or on a different device

  • Tap ☰ → Timer to go back to the Timer screen
  • The system will check the server and restore your active timer automatically
  • If it says "You already have an active timer" but no STOP button appears, try signing out and back in

I see "You already have an active timer" but cannot stop it

  • Go to History, set Status to Active (stuck timers), tap Search
  • If you are an Administrator, use the 🗑 button to delete the stuck record
  • If you are not an Administrator, ask your admin or supervisor to delete or cancel it

A job appears in history but was started by mistake

  • If less than 60 seconds have passed, tap Cancel on the active timer panel
  • If more than 60 seconds have passed, a Supervisor must cancel it — they will need to enter a reason
  • Administrators can delete the record entirely from History if required

The page is not loading

  • Check the tablet is connected to Wi-Fi
  • Pull the page down to refresh
  • If the problem continues, the server may be briefly restarting — wait 30 seconds and try again
